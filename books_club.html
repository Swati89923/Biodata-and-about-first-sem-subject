Book Club

A Book Club is a group of people who gather regularly to discuss a chosen book, sharing thoughts, opinions, and insights.

Book Club Structure

  • Chosen Book: The Alchemist
  • Set a reading schedule
  • Prepare discussion questions
  • Meet to discuss
  • Assignment Questions

Book Club Goals

  • Read widely and diversely
  • Develop critical thinking
  • Improve communication skills
  • Explore new authors and genres

Benefits of Book Club

  • Encourages reading and exploration of new genres
  • Fosters critical thinking and analytical skills
  • Develops communication and discussion skills
  • Builds social connections and community
  • Enhances empathy and understanding through diverse perspectives
